## Optimize Your Day Like a Pro

Transform your chaotic to-do list into a laser-focused schedule. This prompt applies proven productivity frameworks to your specific needs.

### Features

- **Time Blocking**: Strategic allocation of your hours
- **Energy Management**: Tasks aligned with your peak performance
- **Buffer Time**: Built-in breaks and transition periods
- **Priority Matrix**: Focus on what truly matters

### Productivity Frameworks Used

1. **Eisenhower Matrix**: Urgent vs Important
2. **Pomodoro Technique**: Focused work sessions
3. **Parkinson's Law**: Realistic time estimates
4. **Deep Work**: Protected focus blocks

### How to Use

Simply list your tasks and constraints, and get back a customized schedule that:
- Prioritizes high-impact activities
- Groups similar tasks together
- Accounts for your energy levels
- Builds in breaks and recovery time

### Pro Optimization Tips

- Include task complexity levels
- Mention any hard deadlines
- Specify your focus limitations
- Note collaboration requirements
